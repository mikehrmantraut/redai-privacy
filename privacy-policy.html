Introduction

Welcome to Red AI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Generated Content: Images and videos you create using our AI services
  • Text Prompts: The descriptions you enter to generate content
  • Settings Preferences: Your app settings and preferences

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, iOS version, and unique device identifier
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and generation history
  • Technical Data: App version, crash logs, and performance data

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number
  • We do not access your camera roll without permission
  • We do not track your location

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our AI generation services
  • Manage your credits and usage limits
  • Store your generation history for your convenience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure fair usage of our services
  • Analyze app performance and fix technical issues
  • Send important service updates and notifications

2.1 AI Generation Disclaimer

Please Note: The example images and videos displayed in Red AI are for demonstration purposes only. AI-generated content is non-deterministic and results vary with each generation. Using the same prompt multiple times will produce different outputs. We do not guarantee specific results or quality standards.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take your data security seriously:

  • Secure Storage: All data is stored using industry-standard encryption
  • Firebase Firestore: We use Google's Firebase Firestore with security rules to protect your data
  • Access Control: Only you can access your generated content and usage data
  • No Sharing: We never sell your personal information to third parties

4. Third-Party Services

Red AI uses the following third-party services:

4.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Used for: Data storage, authentication, and analytics
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy Policy

4.2 Fal.ai

  • Used for: AI image and video generation
  • Your prompts are sent to Fal.ai's servers for processing
  • Privacy Policy: Fal.ai Privacy Policy

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

5.1 Access Your Personal Data

You can view all your generated content in the "Generations" tab of the app.

Red AI is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hey.redai@gmail.com.
